SPONSORSHIP TIERS

YDCC offers flexible sponsorship packages tailored to meet the unique needs of individuals, small businesses, and larger organisations. Whether you're looking to increase brand visibility or make a meaningful impact in the community, we have a sponsorship tier that aligns with your goals.

Key Highlights:

Multiple Sponsorship Levels: Designed to accommodate different budgets and objectives - from Platinum to Community Partner tiers.

Maximised Exposure: Early confirmation ensures greater visibility across all club promotions throughout the season.

Comprehensive Marketing Campaign: Your brand will be promoted via social media, club emails, newsletters, our website, and at club events and matches.

Simple Onboarding: To confirm your interest, please send us an email at ydccpresident@gmail.com or yerrabidcc@gmail.com.

Join us in shaping the future of cricket in North Canberra while putting your brand in front of a vibrant and growing community.
